    We enable organisations to master their information.

    Founded in 2013, Informotion has specialised in compliance and records management, guiding regulated organisations globally through complexity with clarity, confidence, and proven expertise.

    Today, as data moves to Cloud, AI, and automation, Informotion bridges heritage governance with future-ready innovation. Combining decades of compliance mastery with innovative AI, Cloud, and automation tools, we help organisations transform complex data into actionable insights, wherever they operate.

    What We Do

    Collectively the experience of Informotion’s team goes well beyond 250 years, with many members having worked in the Records and Information Management space for more than 25 years.

    ‌‌ In 2025 Informotion expanded beyond its traditional skill set with the establishment of a highly credentialed Microsoft Cloud & AI practice including Microsoft recognised Most Valuable Professional’s (MVP’s). Today we have experts that span records management through data and information governance to AI.
