    Developing a ministerial briefing system - both usable and secure.

    Informotion partnered with a State Government client to design and develop a ministerial briefing system that was both usable and secure. 

    This State Government department is responsible for providing high quality, timely advice to key ministers and its executives. The preparation of this advice requires consultation and approval across multiple stakeholders and in some circumstances, it must be prepared in critically short timeframes. Our client was utilising their electronic document and records management system (EDRMS) to manage records and briefing documents in conjunction with a separate manual tracking system that required cross-referencing back to the EDRMS. The process was inefficient, presented usability challenges for staff, and increased the departments exposure to risk. 

    Informotion collaborated with the department’s key stakeholders to define the business rules and develop a detailed specification for the development of the solution – key to this was a detailed understanding of the process and desired outcomes. Of critical importance was simplification of user interaction with the EDRMS ensuring documents were created using correct templates, stored in the appropriate file and secured. Visibility of briefs as they progressed through review and approval cycles was identified as a critical requirement to ensure timeframes were met. The solution was developed and iteratively refined in close consultation with key stakeholders and process specialists to deliver a successful outcome.
